About Safety Services

Who We Are?

Safety Services was established in 2000, we are a family run company operating out of Athlone town providing vital health and safety courses to a variety of different sectors including construction and manufacturing. Michael Snr is the owner of Safety Services and is a Ph.D. Human Factors, Occupational Safety and Health, with Michael Jnr a PhD in Occupational Safety and Health.

What We Do?

Safety Services offers on site training and support that would include forklift training, first aid training and manual handling. We also offer these services off site at our state of the art headquarters with the latest technologies.

Why We Do It?

We are dedicated to providing the proactive management of safety and health in the workplace to help organizations prevent injuries and bad health at work. It is important to us heere at Safety Services that employees and employers return from work as safe as they arrived at work.
